# Card 5: 30B+ Models for RTX 3090 (24GB)

## Budget Calculation
- VRAM: 24GB
- KV cache at 256K: ~5.1GB (10 layers × 2 KV heads × 256 dim × 2 bytes × 2 × 262144)
- Overhead: ~2GB (buffers, CLIP projector, scratch)
- **Available for weights: ~17GB**

**Qwen 3.6-35B-A3B at Q3_K_S** — the only 35B+ model with full tool+thinking support that fits on a single 3090 with 256K context. Already owned, just needs re-quantization from the official blob.
